Welcome

Ngano Secondary School is a mixed day and boarding, sub-country school located in Ngano, near Ngano Shopping Centre. It is one of the 11 sub-county schools in Gathanji sub-county, Nyandarua county.

The school stands out as the school of choice for many students within the region, due to its above average academic performance, exemplary performance in both games and music.

An aspiration for all and sundry, here students are shaped to be all-rounded and grounded in character, discipline and academic excellence.

Our History

On 2nd February 1987,the foundation stone was laid by John M. Njuguna, then District Education Officer(D.E.O), Nyandarua District. The school was named Ngano Secondary School after the Ngano sub-location, which is located in the Gathanji Sub-County of Nyandarua County.

Located along the KALRO-Charagita Road, approximately 1.5km from Gathanji town the school was started by the initiative of the local community, as a day school offering the 8-4-4 curriculum.

Ngano has since grown in both size and reputation, attracting students from the surrounding areas, producing notable alumni who have excelled in various fields.

Boarding and CBE

On January 2022, boarding section for both boys and girls was introduced with an initial enrolment of 42 students, a population which has since grown to 150+ students.

The introduction of the boarding section has greatly enriched the school's learning environment, allowing for more focused study and extracurricular activities.

Ngano Secondary School has adapted to the changing educational landscape, becoming a cluster 4 institution under the new CBE curriculum offering a wide range of subjects and programs in all the pathways to meet the diverse needs of its students.

The school is committed to providing a holistic education that not only focuses on academic excellence but also promotes character development, leadership skills, and social responsibility among its students.
