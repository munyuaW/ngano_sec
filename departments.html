Exams

From The Head of Department

Examinations play a crucial role in the educational process, providing a means to assess student learning and understanding. Our department is dedicated to ensuring that assessments are fair, comprehensive, and aligned with the curriculum.

Games

From The Head of Department

Physical education and sports play a crucial role in the holistic development of students. Our department is dedicated to promoting physical fitness, teamwork, and sportsmanship through a variety of sports and activities. We believe that participation in sports helps students develop important life skills such as discipline, leadership, and resilience. We encourage all students to get involved, stay active, and strive for excellence in their chosen sports.

Guidance & Counseling

From The Head of Department

When life throws curveballs, we find ourselves in need of a hand to hold on to. While this is the normal human experience, it can be especially challenging for students navigating the complexities and chaos of teenage, adolescence and early adulthood. Our commitment is to provide a safe and supportive environment where students can feel seen, heard, and valued.

Societies

From The Patrons

Clubs and societies offer an alternate avenue for socialization, exploration, creativity, and personal growth outside the traditional classroom setting. Religious Societies foster a sense of community and belonging among students, besides helping them grow spiritually and morally upright. All students are encouraged to actively participate in these groups and make the most of their school experience.

Art and Music

From The Head of Department

Art and music are essential components of a well-rounded education. They foster creativity, self-expression, and cultural appreciation. Our department is dedicated to providing students with opportunities to explore their artistic talents and develop their skills in various mediums. We believe that every student has the potential to be an artist, and we are here to nurture that potential.
